# Introduction

I'm about to finish my college in a month (this article was written around May
2023). And, one generation goes by, another generation comes. Many people that I
know have been asking the question: "Should I go with CS?". CS is a very hot
major. Good money, tech vibes, people treating nerds differently nowadays (I
don't know ¯\\\_(ツ)\_/¯). But there are a lot of hidden things that you have
not heard frequently.

My job is not to convince you to go with CS or not. It's your career and your
future. I'm here to give you some insights that I have learned from my 4 years
of college as a CS student. I will also include some materials and resources
for you to to learn by yourself if you decide to do so.

Please note that this article is based solely on my personal education.
